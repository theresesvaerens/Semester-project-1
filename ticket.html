Guided tour

For groups of more than 6 people, it is possible to book a guided tour of the museum. These tours leave every hour and cost 70 NOK per person, this includes a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us by using the form below to arrange the tour.
