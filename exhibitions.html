Exhibition Spaces

Cosmology

Explore the universe's vast mysteries, the celestial wonders, "The Big Bang", and the journey from stars to galaxies. Our Cosmology exhibition offers insights into the origins and evolution of the cosmos.

Evolution

See our collection of fossils and specimens that showcase the history of life on Earth, from the earliest organisms to the diversity of species we see today.

Biology and Medicine

Learn about the incredible complexity of the human body, medical discoveries, and the science of health. This exhibit offers an up-close look at anatomy and physiology.

Robotics and AI

Discover the world of robots and artificial intelligence, exploring how technology shapes our future, from autonomous machines to smart systems.

Ecology

Explore the interactions between organisms and their environments, and learn about the impact of climate change, conservation, and biodiversity.
